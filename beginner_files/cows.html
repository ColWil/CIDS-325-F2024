The Noble Cows of the Gothic Age

The Role of Cows in Medieval Times

In the Gothic age, cows were considered creatures of great importance. They provided milk, meat, and hides, contributing greatly to the medieval economy and the survival of families in the villages and manors. The cow was a symbol of sustenance and endurance in harsh times.
