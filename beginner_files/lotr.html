The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

The Fellowship of the Ring

The Fellowship of the Ring is the first film in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. Directed by Peter Jackson, it was released in 2001. The story follows Frodo Baggins, a young hobbit who must leave his home to destroy a powerful ring.

The Two Towers

The Two Towers is the second film in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. Released in 2002, it continues the journey of Frodo, Sam, and their companions as they face new challenges and enemies in their quest to destroy the One Ring.

The Return of the King

The Return of the King is the final film in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. Released in 2003, it concludes the journey of Frodo and his friends as they battle the forces of Sauron and attempt to destroy the One Ring once and for all.
